description: Review churn risk with Outlit customer intelligence
---

Run an Outlit churn review for $ARGUMENTS.

If no customer or segment is provided, identify the highest-risk paying or trialing customers first.

Use the Outlit tools to gather evidence before writing the assessment:
- Start with customer lookup or customer discovery.
- Pull recent timeline events and known facts.
- Search customer context for blockers, sentiment, missing activation, renewal, pricing, support, and executive engagement signals.
- Use source lookups when a fact or search result needs evidence.

Return:
- Risk level and short rationale.
- Top churn signals, each tied to evidence.
- Retention actions the team can take this week.
- Open questions or missing data that would change the assessment.
